Meet Us

We are so lucky to have volunteers helping us, and we always want to celebrate successes. We value their commitment, and we are so fortunate to have support from South Gloucestershire Council. They love to reward our volunteers. Our volunteers below have recently been awarded a Gateway Qualification Level 2 Award in Volunteering. We had a virtual award ceremony on 06th August 2021 to recognise their achievements.

Volunteers usually work with us for a year before starting the qualification, which is optional. Further information can be obtained from the Gateway website.

All volunteers can access the Edenred platform to access a range of further benefits. This is the least we can do to thank you for their commitment and time to making South Gloucestershire a beautiful place to live and experience.
